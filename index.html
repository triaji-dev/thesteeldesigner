How collaboration works

TheSteelDesigner.eu acts as your external steel design, analysis, Tekla and consultancy partner. The practice supports in-house teams when workload is high or when deeper expertise is needed in Eurocodes, SAP2000/ETABS analysis and structural behaviour.

We can join at almost any stage of a project – from early concept and global analysis to detailed Tekla modelling, consultancy on existing designs and second opinions before fabrication. The approach is simple: clear assumptions, clear calculations, clear deliverables — fully aligned with your standards and workflows.

1. Brief & documents

You send architectural/structural drawings, project description, and any existing models or standards – including ongoing projects where you need extra support or a second opinion.

2. Scope, model & fee

We define the scope (analysis in SAP2000/ETABS, design, connections, Tekla, consultancy, review), then estimate hours/fee and milestones. You approve before work starts.

3. Analysis, design, detailing & delivery

The structural model is built or refined in SAP2000/ETABS, checks are performed to Eurocodes, and the Tekla model and drawings are aligned with the design. Consultancy and second-opinion feedback are provided clearly, with deliverables: reports, models, drawings and online meetings for clarifications.
