Structural Analysis & Design

Conceptual and detailed design for various steel structures (halls, platforms, mezzanines) using EN 1993, EN 1998, and EN 1991.

  • Global analysis in SAP2000 / ETABS.
  • Serviceability and stability checks.
  • Seismic design awareness (EN 1998).
Connections & Verification

Steel Connection Design & Review

Detailed design and verification of critical steel connections, including independent reviews for quality assurance.

  • Moment-resisting and simple joints.
  • Base plates, splices, and bracings.
  • QA/QC suitable calculation notes.

The Collaboration Process

We integrate seamlessly with your in-house team as your external steel specialist, providing expertise in complex Eurocode, SAP2000/ETABS analysis, and detailing challenges. We ensure clear communication from start to finish.

1. Initial Briefing & Documentation

2. Scope Definition & Proposal

We define the clear scope (design, analysis, Tekla, consultancy), estimate the hours/fee, and set milestones. Work starts upon your approval.

3. Execution & Delivery

Structural modeling, Eurocode checks, and Tekla model alignment are performed. Deliverables include reports, models, drawings, and online clarification meetings.
